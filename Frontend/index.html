      About Job Fit Analyzer

      This tool uses AI to analyze your resume against job descriptions and provide a match score, skill gap analysis, and personalized career roadmap.

      How it works:

      • Upload your resume in PDF format
      • Select a target job role
      • Our NLP engine extracts your skills and experience
      • Semantic matching compares your profile to job requirements
      • Get a match score (0-100%) and actionable recommendations

      Limitations:

      This is a guidance tool, not a guarantee of job success. Results should be used alongside other career development strategies.
